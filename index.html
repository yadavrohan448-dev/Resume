About Me

I am a PhD scholar in Economics at the Indira Gandhi Institute of Development Research, Mumbai. My research interests lie in development economics, rural electrification, welfare delivery, energy access, labour outcomes, and applied public-policy evaluation.

Research Interests

  • Development Economics
  • Rural Electrification and Energy Access
  • Welfare Delivery and Public Policy
  • Labour and Household Outcomes
  • Applied Econometrics

Working Papers and Projects

Targeted Electricity Subsidies and Rural Development: Evidence from India’s RGGVY Program
This project studies the socioeconomic effects of rural electrification and electricity-related welfare interventions in India using household survey data and quasi-experimental methods.

Effects of Electricity Supply Quality on Income in Rural India
This project examines how electricity supply quality affects household income using IHDS data.
